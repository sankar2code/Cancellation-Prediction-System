A/B holdout: did interventions reduce cancellations?

10% of high-risk bookings are randomly held out with no intervention — the only honest way to measure the North Star (counterfactual). Sample: 1,840 bookings.

Intervened bookings cancelled 13.3 percentage points less than the control group — a statistically significant 42% relative reduction.
